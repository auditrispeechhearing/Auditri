Our Services

Hearing Assessment

Comprehensive audiological evaluation for adults and children to identify the type and degree of hearing loss.

Hearing Aid Consultation

Personalised hearing aid recommendations, fitting, and follow-up to ensure comfort and optimal benefit.

Speech & Language Therapy

Therapy for speech, language, voice and fluency issues with evidence-based strategies for all ages.

Tinnitus Management

Assessment and counselling for tinnitus with sound therapy and practical coping strategies.

Pediatric Audiology

Child-friendly assessments, early intervention and parental guidance to support development.

Communication Training

Auditory training and communication strategies to improve listening skills and social participation.

About Auditri

Auditri is a specialised hearing and speech clinic committed to improving communication and quality of life. We combine clinical expertise with compassionate care to create personalised therapy plans. Our clinic offers modern assessment tools, sound-treated rooms, and experienced therapists.
